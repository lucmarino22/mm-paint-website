How We Work

What to Expect When You Hire M&M

We believe homeowners deserve to know exactly what they're getting into before the work starts. Here's how every project runs — no surprises, no shortcuts.

01
Free Estimate

We come to your home, walk through the project, and give you a clear, honest estimate. No pressure, no upselling — just a straight conversation about what your home needs and what it'll cost.

02

Before any paint is opened, we protect your furniture, floors, and fixtures. We patch cracks, sand surfaces, and prime where needed. This is what separates a lasting finish from one that starts failing in a year.

What We Do

Services

Interior or exterior, single room or whole house — we bring the same level of care and preparation to every job, regardless of size.

Interior Painting

Living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens, trim work, ceilings. We prep every surface thoroughly, protect your belongings, and deliver clean lines and a finish that holds up beautifully for years.

Exterior Painting

Your home's exterior takes a beating from New Jersey weather. We power wash, scrape, prime, and caulk before a drop of paint is applied — so the result looks great and actually lasts.

Drywall & Prep Work

Cracks, holes, water stains, old wallpaper removal — we handle all of it before we paint. A smooth, properly prepared surface is the foundation of every quality finish.

Repaints & Refreshes

Updating a room, freshening up a space before a sale, or finally getting around to that project you've been putting off — we're happy to handle jobs of any scope.

Our Story

The Marino Family

M&M Painting was founded in 1995 by brothers Marino and Marino — two tradesmen who believed the work should be done right or not at all. The name M&M comes from their family name, and to this day the business is run by the same family with the same standards they started with.

Over the past three decades, we've painted hundreds of homes across Princeton, Lawrence, West Windsor, Montgomery, and the surrounding communities. Every one of those jobs was built on the same foundation: honest pricing, thorough preparation, quality materials, and a crew that respects the space they're working in.

When you call M&M, you're talking to the people who will actually show up at your home. There's no call center, no middlemen. We walk through your project with you personally, answer every question you have, and make sure you're confident in what's happening in your home from start to finish.

We're proud of the work we do and the relationships we've built in this community. We'd love the opportunity to earn your trust too.
